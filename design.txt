## **Cybersecurity Crash Course: UI/UX Portal Blueprint**

> **Document Purpose:** This architectural blueprint defines the multi-page structure, content distribution, and user flow for transforming the raw cybersecurity notes into an interactive learning portal.

### **Global UI/UX Architecture**

* **Persistent Navigation:** A fixed top header or a sticky left-hand sidebar will remain visible across all pages.
* **Menu Items:** The persistent menu will include links to: Home, Foundations (Month 1), Labs & Scanning (Month 2), Enumeration & Passwords (Month 3), and the Tool Hub.
* **Cross-linking Strategy:** Whenever a specific command, tool, or port is introduced in a theory module, it will feature an inline hyperlink and a contextual "Associated Tools" sidebar widget.
* **Routing Mechanism:** These widgets and links will route the user directly to the specific anchor tag on the `tools.html` page, ensuring theory connects seamlessly to practical reference material.
